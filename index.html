Indian paratha

ingredients:

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Ghee or oil
  • Stuffing (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Mix whole wheat flour, water, and salt to form a dough. Knead until smooth.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them out into circles.
  3. If using stuffing, place it in the center of the circle and fold the edges to seal it.
  4. Cook the paratha on a hot griddle, applying ghee or oil until both sides are golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with yogurt or pickles.

Japanese sushi

ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori sheets
  • Fresh fish or vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger

Procedure:

  1. Cook sushi rice and let it cool.
  2. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat, spread a thin layer of rice over it.
  3. Add fresh fish or vegetables on top of the rice.
  4. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat.
  5. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Italian pasta

ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • herbs

Procedure:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce and basil, simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Combine cooked pasta with the sauce and herbs.

Chicken biriyani

ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Spices
  • Cilantro

Procedure:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook rice until partially done and set aside.
  3. Sauté onions until golden brown, then add marinated chicken and cook until done.
  4. Layer the chicken and rice in a pot, cover, and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  5. Garnish with cilantro before serving.

American sandwich

ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Deli meat or vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Condiments
  • Lettuce and tomato

Procedure:

  1. Layer deli meat or vegetables, cheese, lettuce, and tomato between slices of bread.
  2. Add condiments of your choice (e.g., mayonnaise, mustard).
  3. Close the sandwich and cut it in half if desired.
  4. Serve with chips or a side salad.

Healthy salad

ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Avocado
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil and vinegar dressing

Special note: Avoid adding sugar for health benefits.

Procedure:

  1. Combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, avocado, and feta cheese in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar dressing.
  3. Toss the salad to evenly coat the ingredients with the dressing.
  4. Serve immediately for a fresh and healthy meal.
